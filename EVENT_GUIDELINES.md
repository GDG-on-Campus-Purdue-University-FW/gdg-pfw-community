# Event Organization Guidelines

These guidelines help maintain consistent standards for events organized by GDG on Campus Purdue University Fort Wayne.

## Event Planning

When planning events, organizers should:

- Clearly communicate the purpose of the event
- Specify whether the event is beginner-friendly or requires prior experience
- Provide clear schedules and participation instructions

Transparency helps participants feel more comfortable attending events for the first time.

## Collaboration During Events

Many of our events involve collaborative activities such as hackathons or group projects.

Organizers should encourage:

- Inclusive team formation
- Support for beginners
- Respectful communication among team members

Participants should not be excluded from team activities or ignored during collaboration.

## Communication

Event information should be shared through official community channels such as:

- Discord
- GDG Community platform
- Email announcements
- Social media updates

Clear communication ensures that members know how to participate and stay informed about upcoming activities.

## Safety and Respect

All events must follow the community Code of Conduct.

Organizers should monitor events and be available to respond if any participant experiences discomfort or inappropriate behavior.

## Continuous Feedback

After events, organizers should gather feedback from participants to improve future activities.

Community feedback helps us better understand what works well and what can be improved.
