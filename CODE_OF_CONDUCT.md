# Community Code of Conduct

GDG on Campus Purdue University Fort Wayne is committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment where students can learn, collaborate, and build projects together.

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces including:

- In-person events
- Online platforms such as Discord
- Community discussions and collaborative projects
- Any event organized under GDG on Campus Purdue University Fort Wayne

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

- Treat others with respect and professionalism
- Encourage and support beginners who are new to technology
- Allow space for everyone to contribute to discussions and projects
- Communicate respectfully both online and in person
- Collaborate constructively during events such as hackathons

Our goal is to create an environment where participants feel comfortable asking questions, sharing ideas, and learning from one another.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or intimidation of any participant
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Threats of violence
- Sharing explicit or inappropriate content
- Spamming or disruptive behavior in community channels
- Intentionally excluding others from collaboration during team activities

Any behavior that creates a hostile or unsafe environment for others will be addressed by the organizing team.

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the organizing team.

Reports can be made by:

- Contacting a GDG organizer directly during events
- Messaging organizers through the community Discord server
- Reaching out through official community communication channels

Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed by the organizing team.

## Enforcement

Organizers reserve the right to take appropriate action in response to violations of this Code of Conduct. Actions may include:

- Verbal or written warnings
- Removal from events
- Removal from community platforms
- Restrictions from attending future events

Our goal is not punishment but ensuring that the community remains safe and welcoming for everyone.
